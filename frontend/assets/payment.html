Security

All transactions with Alt.Bet use Transport Layer Security (TLS) and Secure Socket Layer (SSL). Please see Terms and Conditions for more details about your security.

Alt.Bet holds your funds separately from business accounts in dedicated ring-fenced accounts for players. We are required by our license to inform you that such funds are not insured or protected by guarantee, and in the event of the company's insolvency your funds would not be protected. According to the Curacao Gambling Commission's customer funds rating categories, this provides a Basic level of protection.
