Responsible Gambling

Do you have any information on responsible gambling support?

Alt.bet only accepts players of legal gambling age (18). We want members to enjoy gambling. We do not want anyone to suffer any hardship or cause their families or friends to suffer as a result of out of control gambling. alt.bet will help anyone who believes they are developing a gambling problem. We will suspend accounts or impose limits on customers we believe to be showing signs of problem gambling. Alt.bet, however, cannot be held responsible for the actions of its members.
Responsible Gaming tips
For many people gambling is exciting, fun and entertaining, but if you feel you are developing a problem with gambling, here are some suggestions on how you can regain control: Decide on a loss limit ahead of time and stick to it. You can set this on the account limits page. Never borrow money to gamble. Balance gambling with other activities. Don't gamble when highly stressed, depressed or intoxicated. Only gamble with money set aside for entertainment, never with money for everyday expenses.
How to realize you have a problem
If you are leaving your family alone for long periods of time in order to gamble. If you argue frequently with family or friends about spending too much time and money on gambling and find that you cannot stop or limit yourself. If you are gambling money needed for housing, education, health care, food, clothing or utilities. When you are with your family, at work or at social events, you find that you are constantly thinking about your next bet.
Take Action
If you think you have a gambling problem, you should seek help and suspend your account until you resolve your issues. For your benefit, you will not be allowed to rejoin alt.bet for a minimum of six months. If you have any questions you can get in touch with alt.bet through our contact page.

How can I set a deposit or loss limit on my account?

You can set both deposit and loss limits on your account in the Settings. Once your deposit or loss limit has been set there will be a week pending period before an increase of your limits will take effect. Reductions will take effect immediately.

I wish to time out/take a break from using alt.Bet.

You can time out from alt.Bet for one day, seven days or 31 days. During this time you will not be able to bet or use alt.Bet. You can start a time out on your account here.

I wish to self-exclude from using alt.Bet.

You can self-exclude from your account meaning you can no longer access your alt.Bet account for the duration of the exclusion.
The minimum period for a self exclusion is six months. Once your chosen time period has elapsed, you can request your account to be re-opened, which must be completed by phoning our customer support line. You can initiate a self exclusion on your account here.
For exclusions above five years, or to permanently self-exclude, please contact our customer support team.
